Screenwriter

On The Brink – Season 3

Vedant worked as Screenwriter on On The Brink – Season 3 (Hotstar–NatGeo), an upcoming wildlife docudrama spotlighting endangered species across the Indian subcontinent. Episodes from the series have earned official selections and nominations at prestigious environmental and wildlife film festivals worldwide, including the Trento Film Festival, Planet Lens Film Festival, Santiago Wild Festival, Frome International Climate Film Festival, and The Inheritance Film Festival.

Associate Director

An Engineered Dream

Vedant worked as Associate Director on An Engineered Dream, the National Award–winning feature documentary about India’s coaching industry, where he contributed to research, narrative development, second-unit direction, and editing.
